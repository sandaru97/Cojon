Welcome to the BoxDrum! Here’s how to use the app:

  • Click on the pads to play different sounds.
  • Use the record and stop buttons to record your beats.
  • Adjust settings for volume, reverb, and metronome in the settings panel.
  • Toggle the metronome on/off and adjust the BPM using the slider.
